❓ FAQ

Common questions

What is a Groq API key and why do I need it?▼
Groq provides free AI processing. Get your free key at console.groq.com/keys — it takes 30 seconds and is completely free.
Is my data private?▼
Your API key stays only on your device. ClipMind has zero servers. Video transcripts are sent directly to Groq and never stored anywhere.
How is ClipMind different from Eightify or Glasp?▼
Eightify and Glasp only summarize the whole video. ClipMind is the only extension that lets you drag to select a specific clip — much more precise.
What languages are supported?▼
8 languages: English, Bengali (বাংলা), Arabic (العربية), Hindi (हिंदी), Urdu (اردو), Spanish, French, and Turkish.
Does it work on all YouTube videos?▼
Works on any YouTube video with captions available. Videos without captions cannot be summarized.

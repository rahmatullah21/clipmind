Chrome Extension — Privacy Policy

✅ We collect absolutely no personal data

Last updated: February 2025

🔒 Short version: ClipMind does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Your API key stays on your device only. We have no servers.

1. What Data We Collect

Nothing. ClipMind has no servers, no databases, and no backend infrastructure.

2. Your Groq API Key

Stored only on your local device using chrome.storage.local. Never transmitted to ClipMind.

3. YouTube Transcripts

Fetched from YouTube's public API and sent to Groq to generate summaries. Not stored anywhere.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

  • ❌ No analytics
  • ❌ No advertising
  • ❌ No cookies
  • ❌ No user accounts required
